# OpenClaw

- **OpenClaw** (formerly Clawdbot / Moltbot / Molty) is an open-source (MIT-licensed), self-hosted personal AI agent platform. It turns a large language model (like Claude, GPT, or a local model) into a **proactive**, persistent assistant that lives on your machine and works **through the chat** apps you already use (WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, Slack, Signal, iMessage, and many more).
 
- You talk to it in a DM or group chat, and **it can actually do things**: clear your inbox, send emails, manage your calendar, browse the web, run shell commands, edit files, check you in for flights, etc. It runs 24/7 (even when you’re offline), remembers you forever, and can wake itself up on a schedule to be proactive. The entire thing is designed to **keep your data private and under your control**.
