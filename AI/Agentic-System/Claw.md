# Claw

- A "claw" is a personal, always-on AI assistant that lives inside your everyday messaging apps (like WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, or Slack) and actually does real things for you on your computer — not just chats.

## Use-Case

- Imagine you have a super-smart friend who never sleeps, remembers everything about you, and has "hands" to act on your behalf. You message it casually: “Book me a flight to London next week under $800” or “Sort my emails and reply to the urgent ones” or “Check the latest news on AI and summarize it every morning.”
- It wakes up on its own (thanks to a built-in scheduler/heartbeat), thinks step-by-step, uses tools (browse the web, run commands on your computer, control your browser, send emails, manage files, etc.), and gets the job done.
- All of this runs locally on your own hardware (like a Mac Mini, laptop, or cheap VPS), so your data stays private and you’re not locked into a big company’s cloud.

## Features

- Key intuitive traits that make something a claw: Persistent & autonomous
  — It keeps running 24/7 and can act even when you’re offline or asleep.
  - **Messaging-native** — You talk to it the same way you text a friend.
  - **Local-first** — Runs on your machine, with long-term memory stored locally.
  - **Tool-heavy & agentic** — It reasons, plans, and executes multi-step tasks using real tools and permissions you give it.
  - **Open-source & customizable** — You (or the community) can add new “skills” easily.
