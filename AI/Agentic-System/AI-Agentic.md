## AI Agentic
- more often describes the broader capability, paradigm, or property of autonomy and goal-directed behavior. It can refer to the underlying techniques, architectures, or a system-level approach that enables agents (sometimes involving multiple collaborating agents orchestrated together).
- [AI agents]() as the building blocks (task-focused components) and [agentic AI]() as the higher-level framework or orchestration that coordinates them for more complex workflows.
