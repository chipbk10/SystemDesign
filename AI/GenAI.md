## Core Definition & Mechanics

* GenAI is a creator: It is a specialized branch of AI designed to generate entirely new, original content rather than just analyzing or categorizing existing data.
* Pattern recognition: It works by training on massive datasets to learn underlying structures, allowing it to predict and generate logical new outputs in response to user prompts.
* Vector space: It does not convert images, audio, or other inputs into text before processing them. Instead, it converts all input formats (pixels, text, sound waves) into a shared mathematical "meaning space" using vectors.

## LLMs vs. The Broader GenAI Family

* LLMs are just one type: Large Language Models (like GPT-4 or Claude) are a specific subset of GenAI focused strictly on processing and generating text and human language.
* Diverse non-text models: GenAI is a broad umbrella that includes many other models designed for different mediums, such as:
* Image Models: Turning concepts into visuals (e.g., Midjourney, DALL-E).
   * Video Models: Creating moving footage from descriptions (e.g., Sora, Runway).
   * Audio & Music Models: Generating speech, voices, or full musical tracks (e.g., ElevenLabs, Suno).
   * Code & 3D Models: Building software syntax or virtual 3D assets.

## Multimodal Capabilities

* Flexible inputs: GenAI is no longer text-only. Modern systems are natively multimodal, meaning they can directly accept, understand, and combine text, images, video, and audio simultaneously.
