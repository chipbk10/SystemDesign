The Internal Auto-Scaling Alternative

- **How it works**: The extra servers are active in the pool. During low traffic, you use them to run non-critical, flexible background tasks.
- **The Value**: You use that "wasted" power to run data backups, generate reports, run test environments, or patch other systems. When a peak hit, the system pauses these low-priority tasks and shifts 100% of the power to your live traffic.
- **The Benefit**: You get actual business value out of the electricity and hardware you are paying for, and you constantly prove the servers are healthy.
