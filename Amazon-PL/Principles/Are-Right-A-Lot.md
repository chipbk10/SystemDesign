### Are Right, A Lot – Super Simple

**Short meaning:**  
You make good decisions most of the time.

**Simple words:**  
- You have strong judgment.  
- You use facts + data + logic.  
- You listen to others, then decide fast.  
- You are not perfect, but you are right much more than wrong.

**Easy example:**  
Team argues for 2 hours: red button or blue button?  
Most people guess or pick their favorite color.  
“Are Right, A Lot” person checks real data:  
“Blue button got 22% more clicks last month.”  
Everyone agrees → blue wins.

They don’t guess. They find truth fast and are usually correct.  
That’s Are Right, A Lot.
