### Invent and Simplify – Super Simple

**Short meaning:**  
Make new things AND make everything easier.

**Simple words:**  
- Don’t copy old ways.  
- Invent better solutions.  
- Then remove steps, remove complexity, make it simple for everyone.

**Easy example:**  
Old way: Customers fill 10-page form to return a product.  
Most people: “That’s how it’s always been.”  

Invent and Simplify person:  
1. Invents one-click returns with a QR code.  
2. Removes all the pages and questions.  

Now it takes 5 seconds instead of 10 minutes.

They invent cool stuff AND make life simple.  
That’s Invent and Simplify.
