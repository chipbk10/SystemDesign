### Hire and Develop the Best – Super Simple

**Short meaning:**  
Only hire people better than you.  
Then help them grow even more.

**Simple words:**  
- Raise the bar every time you hire.  
- Say “no” to good people if they’re not great.  
- Teach, coach, and give chances so everyone gets stronger.

**Easy example:**  
You need a new teammate.  
100 people apply. 10 are “pretty good.”  
Most bosses: “Just pick one, we’re busy.”  

Hire and Develop the Best person:  
“Waits longer, finds the amazing one, hires her.”  
Then gives her big projects and training so she becomes a superstar.

They build a team of champions.  
That’s Hire and Develop the Best.
