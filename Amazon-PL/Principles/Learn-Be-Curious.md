### Learn and Be Curious – Super Simple

**Short meaning:**  
Never stop learning. Always want to know more.

**Simple words:**  
- You are never “done” learning.  
- You ask “Why?” and “How?” all the time.  
- You try new things, read, experiment, copy the best ideas from everywhere.

**Easy example:**  
New tech comes out (like AI tools).  
Most people: “I’m too busy, I’ll learn later.”  
Learn and Be Curious person:  
Spends 30 min every day playing with it, teaches the team, and finds 3 ways to make work faster.

They stay hungry to grow every day.  
That’s Learn and Be Curious.
