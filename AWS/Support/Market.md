**AWS Marketplace** is an **online store** that helps you **find, buy, and deploy third-party software and services** that run on AWS.

### ✅ **Key Features**

*   Offers **pre-configured software** (e.g., security tools, databases, machine learning solutions).
*   Includes **free, paid, and BYOL (Bring Your Own License)** options.
*   Supports **one-click deployment** to your AWS environment.

***

### ✅ **Example**

A company needs a firewall solution → Instead of building one, they can purchase a **Fortinet firewall** from AWS Marketplace and deploy it directly on EC2.

***

### ✅ **Use Cases**

1.  **Security & Compliance**: Firewalls, intrusion detection systems.
2.  **Data & Analytics**: BI tools, data visualization software.
3.  **DevOps**: CI/CD tools, monitoring solutions.
4.  **Machine Learning**: Pre-trained models and ML frameworks.
