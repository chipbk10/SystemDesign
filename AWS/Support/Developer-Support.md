### ✅ **AWS Basic Support**

*   **Included for free** with every AWS account.
*   **Features**:
    *   24/7 access to **customer service** for billing and account issues.
    *   Access to **AWS documentation**, whitepapers, and forums.
    *   **AWS Trusted Advisor**: Limited checks (service limits and security basics).
*   **No technical support** for troubleshooting workloads.
*   **Best for**: Learning, experimenting, or running non-critical workloads.

***

### ✅ **AWS Developer Support**

*   **Paid plan** for early-stage or development environments.
*   **Features**:
    *   **Email support** during business hours.
    *   Guidance on **best practices** for development.
    *   **Response times**:
        *   General guidance: <24 hours
        *   System impaired: <12 hours
    *   **Trusted Advisor**: Limited checks (same as Basic).
*   **Pricing**: Starts at **$29/month**.
*   **Best for**: Developers building and testing apps, not yet in production.

***

### **Key Difference**

*   **Basic** = Free, billing help only.
*   **Developer** = Adds technical guidance for development, but not 24/7 or production-level support.
