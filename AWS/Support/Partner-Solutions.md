### **AWS Partner Solutions**

*   **What it is:**  
    Pre-built, validated solutions from AWS Partners that solve specific business or technical problems.
*   **Focus:**  
    Ready-to-deploy architectures, often published in the **AWS Solutions Library**.
*   **Use case:**  
    If you want a **tested blueprint** for workloads like data lakes, security, or migration.

***

### **APN Technology Partners**

*   **What it is:**  
    Companies that build **software or hardware products** that integrate with AWS services.
*   **Focus:**  
    Technology offerings (e.g., SaaS apps, security tools, DevOps platforms) that run on or work with AWS.
*   **Use case:**  
    If you need **third-party tools** that complement AWS (e.g., Datadog for monitoring, Snowflake for data warehousing).
