## ✅ Compliance Programs
- **What they are**: AWS certifications and frameworks that show **AWS meets industry and government security standards**.
- **Purpose**: Help customers meet regulatory requirements when using AWS.
- **Examples**:
  - **ISO** 27001 – Information security management
  - **SOC 1, SOC 2, SOC 3** – Service organization controls
  - **PCI DSS** – Payment card data security
  - **HIPAA** – Healthcare data protection
  - **GDPR** – EU data privacy compliance

## Artifacts
- AWS **Artifacts** is to store reports and certifications
- ✅ Who uses it? Your company (AWS customer) uses AWS Artifact to:
  - Download compliance reports for **audits**.
  - Accept compliance-related agreements.
  - Demonstrate compliance to regulators or internal teams.
