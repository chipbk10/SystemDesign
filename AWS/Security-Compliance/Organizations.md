### ✅ **AWS Organizations in one line**

**Central tool to manage multiple AWS accounts with shared policies, billing, and governance.**

***

### ✅ **Best Practices (easy to remember)**

1.  **Secure root accounts** (MFA, shared email, only use root for initial setup & critical recovery action).
2.  **Use multiple accounts** (separate prod/dev, workloads).
3.  **Group accounts into OUs (groups)** (by environment or function).
4.  **Apply SCPs (permissions)** (restrict services and actions).
5.  **Enable org-wide services** (CloudTrail, GuardDuty).
6.  **Centralize billing & tagging**
  - 6.1 consolidate billing for all accounts under one management (payer) account
  - 6.2 track costs and resources based on tags).
