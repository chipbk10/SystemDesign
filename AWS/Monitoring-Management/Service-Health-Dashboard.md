### ✅ **AWS Health**

*   Shows **personalized health information** for **your AWS resources and services**.
*   Example: If an EC2 instance in your account is impacted by a hardware issue, AWS Health will notify you.

***

### ✅ **Service Health Dashboard (SHD)**

*   Public dashboard showing the **status of all AWS services globally**, regardless of whether you use them.
*   Useful for checking **general AWS outages** or regional issues.

***

**Key Difference:**

*   **AWS Health = personalized alerts for your account.**
*   **SHD = global AWS service status for everyone.**
