### ✅ Operational Excellence (AWS Well-Architected Pillar)

**Definition:**  
Operational Excellence focuses on **running and monitoring systems effectively**, and continuously improving processes and procedures. It’s about automation, observability, and making operations repeatable and reliable.

***

### ✅ Key Practices

*   Automate changes and deployments.
*   Respond to events quickly.
*   Document and refine operational procedures.

***

### ✅ AWS Services That Help Achieve Operational Excellence

*   **AWS CloudFormation** – Automate infrastructure provisioning.
*   **AWS Systems Manager** – Manage and automate operational tasks.
*   **Amazon CloudWatch** – Monitor metrics, logs, and set alarms.
*   **AWS Config** – Track resource configurations and compliance.
*   **AWS Trusted Advisor** – Get best practice recommendations.
